Wilson Miner is a digital designer in San Francisco.

I'm particularly interested in design systems, code as a design tool, and how we shape our world through design.

Recently, I helped The California Sunday Magazine and Pop-Up Magazine bring ambitious storytelling and beautiful photography together online and onstage.

As a product designer at Facebook, I helped develop a unified system of interface design guidelines. I led the product design team at Rdio, and I co-founded EveryBlock, an influential civic journalism startup.

At Apple, I worked on the first comprehensive redesign of apple.com in more than 10 years. Before that, I was one of four people who helped create the Django web framework.
